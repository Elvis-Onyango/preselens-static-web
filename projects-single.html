Nairobi Commercial Complex

Comprehensive electrical and mechanical engineering solutions for a modern commercial development

Electrical Engineering Mechanical Systems Energy Efficiency

Project Overview

The Nairobi Commercial Complex is a state-of-the-art mixed-use development in Westlands, featuring office spaces, retail outlets, and hospitality facilities. Praecellens was entrusted with the complete electrical and mechanical engineering design and implementation for this landmark project.

Our team delivered innovative solutions that balanced aesthetic requirements with functional performance, ensuring energy efficiency and sustainability throughout the complex. The project presented unique challenges due to its scale and the need to integrate multiple building systems seamlessly.

Key Engineering Solutions

  • Designed and implemented a robust electrical distribution system serving 25,000 sqm of commercial space
  • Developed energy-efficient HVAC systems tailored to Nairobi's climate
  • Integrated smart lighting controls with daylight harvesting
  • Implemented water conservation systems throughout the complex
  • Designed fire protection systems meeting international standards

Technical Specifications

Electrical Systems

  • 1.5MW power distribution
  • Backup generator system
  • Smart lighting controls

Mechanical Systems

  • Centralized HVAC system
  • Water-efficient plumbing
  • Fire suppression systems
